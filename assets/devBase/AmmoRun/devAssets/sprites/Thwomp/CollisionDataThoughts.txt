Each bucket on the enemy can have up to 8 different collision states.
0-7
0 = BLACK = no collision

BLACK    == NO COLLISION
MAGENTA  == INSTANT KILL
RED      == Attack Damage 100% (The most damage one hit from this enemy can do)
YELLOW   == Attack Damage 40%
CYAN     == Attack Damage 20%
GREEN    == Attack Damage 10%
BLUE     == Attack Damage 5%
WHITE    == Attack Damage 0% (No damage. But collision is happening still)
